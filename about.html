Key Features

  • Live Bus Timings: Get arrival estimates for the next three buses for every service.
  • Nearby Stops: Automatically detects bus stops within a 2km radius using your device's GPS.
  • Interactive Map: Visualize your location and nearby bus stops on a map for easy navigation.
  • Instant Refresh: Update arrival times with a single tap to get the latest data.
  • Mobile-Friendly: A clean, dark-mode interface that's easy to read day or night.

How It Works

  1. Allow Location Access: For the best experience, grant permission to use your location.
  2. View Nearby Stops: The app will immediately display a list of the closest bus stops.
  3. Check Timings: See upcoming bus arrivals at a glance for each stop.

About Us

SG Bus Time was born from a simple idea: to make bus travel in Singapore easier and more predictable for everyone. We are a team of passionate developers and daily commuters who understand the frustration of missing a bus by seconds or waiting endlessly without knowing when it will arrive.

Our mission is to provide a fast, reliable, and user-friendly web application that delivers real-time bus arrival information directly to your fingertips. We believe that by leveraging the power of open data from the Land Transport Authority (LTA), we can help you plan your journey better, reduce waiting times, and make your daily commute a little less stressful.
